Aeon

Aeon is a text typeface designed for Dennis Danvers' novel, "Circuit of Heaven". It grew inspiration from the style of Trump Medieval, the fat serifs of FF Dora, and the overall angular shape of Capita Regular. The name "Aeon" and the textface itself is also an homage towards the idea of preserving oneself into a digital realm, an idea that is emphasized within "Circuit of Heaven".
