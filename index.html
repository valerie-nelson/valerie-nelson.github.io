Valerie Nelson is a painter and designer currently studying Graphic Design at the Rhode Island School of Design. She can create both emotional and purely informational work and her favorite things are color, composition, and narrative. She is very fascinated with how the media portrays pop stars, especially the young ones. She also dreams of becoming a popstar, herself, one day. She is also always constantly being nostalgic so it is possible that her childhood memories may emerge from within her school projects. She is allergic to hostility and she can also be corny.
